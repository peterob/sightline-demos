💡 The Key Insight
Capital allocation is not a new primitive — it's a vertical application built on existing CAST primitives. The funding waterfall (Working Capital → Debt → Equity) is a policy rule, not a data model. The only differences between HOA, Startup, and PE are configuration: terminology, time horizons, approval authorities, and constraint thresholds. The structure is identical.
