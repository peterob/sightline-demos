Framework

SaaS Digital Efficiency Framework (PDF)

A step-by-step SaaS adaptation of a digital end-to-end efficiency approach: select a process, analyze pain points, assess solution options, then prioritize a roadmap.

Format: PDF

End-to-End SaaS Processes

End-to-End SaaS Process Library (PDF)

Standard definitions of core SaaS end-to-end processes (e.g., Lead-to-Cash, Order-to-Entitlement, Onboard-to-Value, Support-to-Resolution, Incident-to-Recovery) to align scope and KPIs.

Format: PDF

Select Process

Process Selection Criteria (PDF)

Criteria to identify the end-to-end SaaS process with the greatest opportunity for efficiency, growth impact (ARR/NRR/churn), reliability, or compliance improvement.

Format: PDF

Process Selection Scorecard (XLSX)

A weighted 1–5 scoring worksheet to compare candidate processes and rank where to start. Includes adjustable weights and scoring guidance.

Format: XLSX

Process Overview Visualization Template (PPTX)

A simplified visual template to capture stages, roles, systems, and handoffs—useful for aligning stakeholders before deep analysis.

Format: PPTX

Stakeholder Interview Guide Template (DOCX)

A structured interview guide to understand current state, surface pain points, collect evidence, and validate methods across Sales, CS, Support, Engineering, Finance, and Security.

Format: DOCX

Analyze Process

Journey Map and Persona Template (PPTX)

Create representative SaaS personas (Buyer/Admin/End User) and journey maps (Discover → Activate → Adopt → Renew) to document goals, needs, frustrations, and success metrics.

Format: PPTX

Pain Point Catalog Template (XLSX)

Capture pain points with evidence, baseline metrics, frequency/severity, root-cause category, systems involved, and ownership—so patterns can be traced and referenced.

Format: XLSX

Pain Point Down-Select Tool (XLSX)

Score and down-select pain points aligned to SaaS priorities (ARR/churn/CX/reliability) vs delivery cost and complexity.

Format: XLSX

Assess Solutions

Solution Category Decision Tree (PDF)

A guide to categorize solutions as Reduce/Optimize (simplify/remove work), Digitize/Automate (self-serve/workflows/integrations), or Innovate/Intelligent (AI/predictive/AIOps/new capability).

Format: PDF

Solution Prioritization Tool (XLSX)

Prioritize pain point + solution combinations using weighted value vs cost scoring and a 2x2 quadrant (best bets, consider carefully, etc.).

Format: XLSX

Prioritize Solutions

Transformation Blueprint Template (PPTX)

Visualize key process pain points and associated solution opportunities, then translate them into a quarterly roadmap and KPI targets.

Format: PPTX
