Phase Plan

PoC → Pilot, Without Heavy Integration

Uploads → Exports

We sequence Sightline to avoid integration drag. Phase 1 proves value with file uploads and manual mapping. Phase 2 automates the same flows via scheduled exports from your existing tools.

1

Diagnostic PoC (4–6 weeks)

Uploads + manual mapping
  • Ingest Shopify, PO/ERP, 3PL, AP, and bank CSVs.
  • Build PO→receipt→invoice→payment chains for a representative slice.
  • Quantify early/no-proof payments, mismatches, and vendor exposure.
2

Early Product Pilot (8–12 weeks)

Scheduled exports
  • Set up daily/weekly exports from Shopify, ERP, 3PL, AP, and bank.
  • Run agreed control pack on new flows; surface exceptions daily.
  • Provide dashboards and "power user" investigation views.
Data in, risk out: Sightline only needs flat files or exports; it rebuilds your world as PO→shipment→receipt→invoice→payment→order events with controls evaluated on top.
Control Packs

Policy Modules That Move the Risk Map

CFO · Lender-Friendly

Each control pack is a versioned "contract" over your PO-to-cash events. We start with a small set that directly moves exposures up-and-left on the map.

AP Core
3/4-Way Match & Pay Window
Blocks payments without matching PO + receipt (within tolerance), and aligns pay dates with receipt + terms.
Impact: ↑ realization, ↓ early cash burn
Physical Proof
Possession Before Payment
Enforces "no cash out until physical control consistent with Incoterms" using 3PL receipts and logistics milestones.
Impact: ↑ lender confidence, ↓ bottom-right exposure
Vendor Risk
Exposure & KYB Guardrails
Caps vendor exposure, requires KYB/terms attestations, and routes exceptions into a structured queue.
Impact: ↑ diversification, ↓ concentrated risk
Margin Integrity
Landed Cost vs Sell-Through
Links PO cost + freight to realized selling price by SKU/lane, and surfaces top margin leakage patterns.
Impact: ↑ unit economics, ↓ unobserved erosion
