Built for measurable outcomes

We help teams scale with confidence by aligning finance staffing to complexity, capital structure, and operating cadence.

What We Assess

  • Team structure, role coverage, and decision rights
  • Depth across controllership, FP&A, cash management, and reporting
  • Capacity constraints, execution bottlenecks, and key-person risk
  • Alignment between staffing and business complexity

What You Get

  • Objective capability assessment (current state)
  • Prioritized gaps tied to business and reporting risk
  • Sequenced 6–12 month hiring and investment roadmap
  • Role definitions focused on outcomes and accountability

When sponsors and CEOs deploy this

Most often used at moments of increased complexity or risk.

  • Fundraising or capital raise preparation
  • Adding leverage or increasing covenant sensitivity
  • ERP, reporting, or data model transformation
  • Missed forecasts or reduced board confidence
  • CFO transition, succession planning, or role re-scoping

Typical Outcomes

Avoid premature or mis-scoped senior hires. Improve forecast reliability and board reporting confidence. Reduce dependency on individual contributors. Ensure finance headcount scales with complexity—not just revenue.
