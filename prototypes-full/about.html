Our Story

Digital Finance HQ was founded to bring senior-level financial expertise to companies that need it most—those navigating critical inflection points like rapid growth, acquisitions, or operational transformation.

Our founder has served as CFO at companies including Motivate (the largest bike share operator in North America) and Food52, and has advised over 40 companies on financial operations and systems as Senior Advisor at Norwest.

We've seen what works and what doesn't across industries—from ecommerce and SaaS to complex supply chain operations. This breadth of experience allows us to bring battle-tested solutions to your specific challenges.
