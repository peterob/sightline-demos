Events & Journals: The Foundation

Not all events lead to journals — but every journal has an event. This is how we preserve context, enable traceability, and make your financial data tell its complete story.

What This Architecture Unlocks
The "Why" Is Always Attached
Every journal entry links to its source event. Every event carries its metadata — who approved it, what triggered it, which contract it traces to. No more archaeology through emails and spreadsheets.
See Before Accounting Happens
Commitments exist the moment a PO is approved. Terms changes are recorded when negotiated. Your forecast updates immediately, not when the journal posts weeks later.
Non-Accounting Events Matter
A terms change isn't a journal entry. But it affects cash timing, forecast accuracy, customer context. In Sightline, it's part of the chain — visible, traceable, never lost.
