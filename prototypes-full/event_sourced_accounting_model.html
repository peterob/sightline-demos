The Ledger Engineer's Insight

Traditional accounting stores journal entries as the source of truth and discards the underlying events. This is like physics storing only velocity while discarding position and acceleration—you lose the ability to reconstruct what actually happened. Event-sourcing inverts this: store the events, compute everything else. Journal entries become views. Account balances become projections. Financial statements become aggregations. The same event stream can produce cash basis, accrual basis, or any other valid interpretation—deterministically and auditably.
