Source Field Name Data Type Description Formula / Logic Used In
Revenue & Collections
PMS gross_production DECIMAL Total value of procedures completed at full fee schedule rates before any adjustments SUM(procedure.fee_amount) Dashboard, P&L
PMS net_production DECIMAL Production after contractual adjustments and write-offs gross_production - adjustments - writeoffs Dashboard, P&L
PMS patient_payment DECIMAL Direct patient payments including copays, deductibles, and self-pay amounts SUM(payment WHERE source='patient') Dashboard, AR
INS insurance_payment DECIMAL Payments received from insurance carriers via ERA/EFT SUM(era.payment_amount) Dashboard, AR
CALC total_collections DECIMAL Combined patient and insurance payments received patient_payment + insurance_payment Dashboard, KPIs
CALC collection_rate PERCENT Percentage of net production collected within reporting period (total_collections / net_production) × 100 Dashboard, KPIs
Accounts Receivable
PMS ar_current DECIMAL Receivables aged 0-30 days from date of service SUM(balance WHERE age_bucket='0-30') AR Aging, Dashboard
PMS ar_31_60 DECIMAL Receivables aged 31-60 days from date of service SUM(balance WHERE age_bucket='31-60') AR Aging
PMS ar_61_90 DECIMAL Receivables aged 61-90 days from date of service SUM(balance WHERE age_bucket='61-90') AR Aging
PMS ar_over_90 DECIMAL Receivables aged over 90 days - high collection risk SUM(balance WHERE age_bucket='90+') AR Aging, Alerts
CALC ar_total DECIMAL Total outstanding accounts receivable ar_current + ar_31_60 + ar_61_90 + ar_over_90 Dashboard, Balance Sheet
CALC days_sales_outstanding INTEGER Average number of days to collect payment after service (ar_total / (net_production / days_in_period)) Dashboard, KPIs
Insurance Claims
INS claims_submitted INTEGER Count of claims sent to clearinghouse/carriers COUNT(claim WHERE status='submitted') Claims Dashboard
INS claims_accepted INTEGER Claims accepted by carrier for processing COUNT(claim WHERE status='accepted') Claims Dashboard
INS claims_denied INTEGER Claims rejected by carrier - requires follow-up COUNT(claim WHERE status='denied') Claims Dashboard, Alerts
CALC clean_claim_rate PERCENT Percentage of claims accepted on first submission (claims_accepted / claims_submitted) × 100 KPIs
INS claim_amount DECIMAL Dollar value submitted on claim SUM(claim.billed_amount) Claims Dashboard
INS claim_paid_amount DECIMAL Actual payment received from carrier SUM(era.payment_amount) Claims Dashboard, AR
Expenses & Payables
ERP supplies_expense DECIMAL Dental supplies and materials purchased SUM(expense WHERE category='supplies') P&L, Budget
ERP lab_expense DECIMAL Dental laboratory fees for crowns, dentures, etc. SUM(expense WHERE category='lab') P&L, Budget
PAYROLL payroll_expense DECIMAL Total payroll including wages, benefits, taxes gross_wages + employer_taxes + benefits P&L, Budget
PAYROLL fte_count INTEGER Full-time equivalent employee count SUM(hours_worked / 2080) Staffing, Budget
ERP rent_expense DECIMAL Monthly facility rent/lease payments SUM(expense WHERE category='rent') P&L, Budget
CALC total_opex DECIMAL Total operating expenses supplies + lab + payroll + rent + utilities + marketing + other P&L, Dashboard
Banking & Cash
BANK bank_balance DECIMAL Current available balance per bank feed Direct feed from bank API Cash Dashboard
BANK deposits DECIMAL Total deposits credited to account SUM(txn WHERE type='credit') Cash Dashboard, Recon
BANK withdrawals DECIMAL Total withdrawals/payments from account SUM(txn WHERE type='debit') Cash Dashboard, Recon
CALC cash_flow_net DECIMAL Net change in cash for period deposits - withdrawals Cash Dashboard
CALC days_cash_on_hand INTEGER Days of operations current cash can cover bank_balance / (total_opex / days_in_period) Dashboard, Alerts
