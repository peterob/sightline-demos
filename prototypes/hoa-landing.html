The Real Problem

Most HOAs have written policies. Two signatures required. Board approval for large expenses. Reserve funds protected. Separation of duties.

But those policies are manual. They live in bylaws, not in software. Payments are recorded after they occur. Controls are reconstructed after loss.

That gap is how incidents happen.

Why Status Quo Software Fails

  • Records transactions but does not enforce approval thresholds.
  • Provides permissions but does not prevent self-approval.
  • Generates reports but does not block improper payments.
  • Logs activity but cannot prevent policy bypass.

Most systems document what happened. They do not prevent what should not happen.

What We Do Differently

We install an enforceable control layer into your financial workflow.

  • Payments are automatically evaluated against written rules.
  • Large expenses require enforced multi-party approval.
  • Vendor bank changes trigger mandatory review.
  • Reserve and escrow releases cannot bypass required votes.
  • Every decision is logged with the rule version that applied.

Controls move from policy documents to executable enforcement.
