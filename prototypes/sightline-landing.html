THE PROBLEM

One person controls the books.
Everyone else stays in the dark.

In HOAs and small organizations, treasurers manage millions with little oversight. Edits go unnoticed, approvals are verbal, and fraud risks are high. Residents lose trust — and money.

SIGHTLINE SOLVES IT
  • 🔒
    Tamper-proof hashes
    Every transaction is cryptographically locked the moment it’s entered.
  • 👥
    Multi-approval workflow
    No single person can move money without documented sign-off.
  • 📊
    Live Trust Score + Integrity Check
    Real-time visibility for every board member and resident.
FEATURES

Built for real transparency

🔐

SHA-256 Tamper Detection

Any change after entry is instantly flagged. End-to-end proof of causality from proposal to payment.

✅

Multi-Party Approvals

Require 2+ independent approvals with timestamps and notes. Rejections are visible to everyone.

📈

Gamified Trust Score

Live percentage based on approved transactions. Hit 100% and celebrate “Perfect Transparency.”
