EXP-017 Ready for review
Raise SaaS auto-approve threshold to $2,500
Analysis of 6 months of SaaS invoices shows 94% of items between $2,000-$2,500 are recurring, low-risk subscriptions that were manually approved without changes. Raising the threshold would eliminate ~40 manual reviews per month.
Tested on 847 txns
False positive 0.3%
Time saved 12 hrs/mo
Confidence 97%
