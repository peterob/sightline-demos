🎯 Core User Story

As an HOA resident, I want to verify that our $3,500 landscaping payment was properly approved and executed, without having to trust the board, accountant, or property manager.

🛡️ Trust Problem

Multiple organizations handle our money, volunteers change frequently, and we need mathematical proof that policies are followed - not just promises.
